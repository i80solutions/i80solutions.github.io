About

i80solutions is a personal research initiative exploring Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) in high-trust, closed-domain applications. The focus is on precision-first architecture, fallback logic, and language-aware embeddings that help AI systems avoid hallucination.

I started i80solutions to deepen the practical understanding of RAG systems and Large Language Grounding (LLG) in private-domain environments. Through hands-on experiments, log-driven insights, and systematic evaluations, I explore how RAG can evolve into a reliable foundation for real-world applications — where accuracy, control, and contextual integrity matter most.

— Alex Wang
October 29, 2024
